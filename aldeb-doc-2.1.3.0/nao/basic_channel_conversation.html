Languages¶

You can ask NAO witch languages he can speak and understand.

English French
What languages do you speak? Quelles langues parles-tu?

You can even ask him to change his current settings.

However, it is not recommended to switch the language setting while the Autonomous Life is on. To change the settings safely, prefer the NAO Web page while the Autonomous Life is off.

Speak French.  
Can you speak French?
